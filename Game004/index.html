Galaxy Escape

  • Originality: An elevator/bridge like obstacle concept that challenges players' quick decision-making skills.
  • Technical Merit: Varied jump height, double-jump, top/bottom/sidewall touch detection, scrolling/fading text, multiple sprite movement control, collision, scroll style gameplay, time slow
  • Prototype Postmortem: Learned the sprite scale, health bar display, and camera control.

  • High Concept: Guide your chicken through the 5 levels of space debris that can crush you in between.
  • Theme: Iteration of week 2 game, "Farm Escape".
  • Mandated Variety: Input-keyboard, Randomness-A lot of randomness, Play styles-achiever, Genre:playformer
  • Prototype Goal: The goal is to reach level 5; each level gets harder and will require significant skills to pass.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players are expected to quickly become familiar with game mechanics. Their experiences with the game should be closely connected with their skills; the more skills they develop, more levels they will clear.
  • Gameplay: Player will need up, right, left keys through most of their gameplay, left and right keys move the chicken in those directions, and up key will make the chicken jump. Jump height can be controlled by keeping the up key pressed. Player can also press jump again to double-jump. If player's chicken runs in between two logs closing into each other, the chicken will get crushed and it is game over. Each advance in stage will increase the speed and number of logs passing through the stage. Press S key to slow down time to perform exceptional maneuvers. However, slowing down time will consume energy, which will recharge over time. Player's chicken will die if it falls into the bottom of screen or crushed between the logs. Pressing Enter will restart the game.
  • Strategies: Players can keep their chicken in the upper half of the screen to avoid falling into the pit. Looking ahead of chicken's path is key!
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Game is set in a galactic zone of space junks. Player is a space chicken cadet whose mission is to navigate through the space junks. Its time-slowing ability will help guide its fate in its survival.
  • Target Audience: This game is mainly made for anyone that enjoys a fast, non-serious gameplay, most-likely from kids to young adults.
  • Play Time: 1 to 3 minutes
