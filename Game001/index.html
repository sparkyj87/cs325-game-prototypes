Player will need the 4 arrow keys through most of their gameplay, left and right keys turn player's ship, and up and down keys control forward and backward movement of the ship. Turning has instant acceleration, whereas movement has gradual acceleration. Each stage puts player's ship in the left safe-zone, and player will simply guide his ship to the right safe-zone to move onto the next stage. Each increase in stage will raise the difficulty, either by raising the speed or the number of mines. When player dies or finishes stage 18, game over message appears and the player can press the space bar to restart.
  • Strategies: When the mines start increasing numbers after each stage try not to wait too long for an opening, because some mines will gain speed by bouncing off other mines. They can move faster than player's ship if they gain the momentum. Also, sometimes navigating along the map edges can help dodge the mines.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Game is set in a golf course without gravity. Player character is the golf-ball that wants to survive through the 18-hole filled with exploding mines. No backstory is provided in this game. The increasing difficulty represents the main tension of this prototype.
  • Target Audience: This game is mainly made for anyone that enjoys short, fast-paced game, most-likely from kids to young-adults.
  • Play Time: 1 to 3 minutes
